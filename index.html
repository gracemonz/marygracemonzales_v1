About

I initially thought a career as a UI/UX Designer was my calling when I enrolled as a computer science student, driven by my love for creating aesthetically pleasing and functional web applications. As an artist at heart, I found joy in the design process. But what I didn't anticipate was the fun in both designing and making those designs work through development. Curiosity nudged me into the world of feature development and web solutions, launching my career as a PHP Developer in 2018. What began as a curious detour has now become my main passion.

I've delved into various technologies, building SaaS for advertising, real estate, retail, payment solutions, and logistics. These days, I focus on developing and maintaining features for Trunkrs' in-house applications to keep their operations running smoothly.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me exploring new restaurants, travelling locally, free diving, or playing games.
